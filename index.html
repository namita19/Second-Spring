Built by Women, For Women

Second Spring was born from lived experience. As women who've navigated perimenopause ourselves, we understood the gap between what women need and what's available.

We combined our backgrounds in product management and medical expertise to create a solution that doesn't just manage symptoms—it empowers women to thrive during this transformative time.

Our mission is to shift the cultural narrative from silence and struggle to empowerment and renewal, creating a world where every woman has the tools she needs for her Second Spring.
